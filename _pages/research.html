Project 1: Hypervelocity Impact Loading into Competent Rocks

This research aims to understand the non-linear behavior of geomaterials under high-stress conditions typically found in deep underground environments. We utilize advanced computational methods (e.g., FEM, DEM) coupled with novel experimental techniques.

  • Key Goal: Develop constitutive models for large-scale geotechnical systems.
  • Method: Integrating Machine Learning with classic mechanics principles.
  • Sponsor: National Science Foundation (NSF).
